Colombia

Official Flag of Colombia

Colombia is a wonderful country. It has different types of weather. Colombia is located on the equator line, which is why we don not have seasons. We have a tropical climate. In each city, we can experience different temperatures. Even though we don not have snow in most places, we have a mountain called Nevado del Ruiz where it actually snows. We also have amazing landscapes such as the River of Seven Colors and the Tatacoa Desert. Colombia has great diversity in plants and animals, and our food is delicious.
